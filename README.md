## üéÆ Core Mechanics

### Unified Resource System: Blood is Fuel
Velkan does not possess a traditional mana bar. Instead, he utilizes **Blood Arts**‚Äîpowerful spells that burn his own **Health (HP)** to cast. Every spell cast is a calculated risk.

### The Aggression Loop
The only way to heal is through violence. Passive regeneration does not exist.
*   **Siphon:** Executing stunned enemies restores a large, critical chunk of Blood (HP).
*   **Reaping:** Standard melee hits restore small amounts of "Temporary Blood" (which regains over time if you avoid damage).

### Movement
*   **Dash:** A high-speed horizontal dodge granting Invincibility Frames (I-Frames).
*   **Vault:** A context-sensitive action to hop over gaps or low cover (No free jumping).

## üèõÔ∏è Design Pillars

### Darkness, Despair, and Vengeance
The atmosphere reflects the bleak reality of a fallen kingdom. Every visual and auditory element reinforces Velkan's drive for revenge against overwhelming odds.

### Narrative: Fragmented & Discovery-Driven
The deep backstory is not spoon-fed but uncovered through exploration:
*   **Environmental Storytelling:** Ruined architecture and the banners of invaders tell the history of the siege.
*   **Memory Echoes:** Interact with "Blood Stains" to witness phantom replays of how citizens met their fate.
*   **Survivor Dialogue:** Rescuing key survivors provides human context and current objectives.

### Progression & Power-ups
Players can customize their playstyle significantly through **Blood Arts**. Whether focusing on brutal melee combos or devastating blood magic, the choice is yours.

### Satisfying Gameplay
Combat is designed to generate a feeling of power and agility. Success requires mastering combos, evading traps with nimble movement, and traversing the map with speed and precision.
